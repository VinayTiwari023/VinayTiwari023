## 🚀 Projects I've Shipped

> All 6 apps below are **live on Play Store** from my time at Seabird Logisolutions

---

### 📦 WMS — Warehouse Management System
*Flutter · BLoC · Zebra EMDK · DataWedge · ESC/POS · ZPL*

Full-cycle warehouse app covering inward, grading, picklist, challan, and POD flows. Integrated Zebra HHT scanners for **500+ daily scans at 99.9% accuracy**, and thermal label printing at **1,000+ labels/day**. Replaced 70% of manual entry at Vantara warehouses.

---

### 🚛 TMS — Transport Management System
*Flutter · BLoC · REST APIs · Firebase*

Automates **20+ vehicle dispatches daily** — trip planning, driver assignment, and live POD capture. Built to reduce turnaround time and give dispatchers real-time fleet visibility.

---

### 🤝 WMS Vendor Portal
*Flutter · Provider · REST APIs*

Separate vendor-facing app (distinct from WMS) for PO tracking, inward confirmation, and challan management. Role-scoped and lean — vendors get exactly what they need, nothing more.

---

### 🏗️ ERP + NLS HHT Integration *(SKL — In Production)*
*Android · Java · Kotlin · NLS SDK · Newland HHT · MVVM*

Enterprise ERP app for precast manufacturing, natively integrated with Newland HHT devices via NLS SDK. Covers: material inward → grading → picklist → dispatch. Currently the most technically complex work I've done — native Java bridge, platform channels, custom scan listeners.

---

### 👤 EMS & VMS — Employee & Visitor Management
*Flutter · Provider · Firebase · ML Kit*

QR-based check-in with ML Kit face capture and real-time attendance sync. Deployed across multiple client sites. Taught me a lot about edge-case camera handling on mid-range Android devices.
